HEAD OF DEPARTMENT OF THE DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER SCIENCE

Mr. Jamal Abdulai is the Head of Department. He is a Senior Lecturer. He received the BSc degree in Computer Science from the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST), Kumasi, Ghana in 1991, his MSc Computer Science and Applications degree from Shanghai University of Science and Technology, Shanghai, China in 1996 and his PhD from the Southampton University, Southampton, England in 2003. He is currently a Senior Lecturer at the Department of Computer Science, Legon. He has over 50 publications to his credit. His main research areas include Biometrics, Cloud Computing, Image Processing and Computer Vision.

Institution

The University of Ghana is one of the Africa's most preeminent public universities. Our impact on individuals, our region and the world is profound. whether we are launching young people into a boundless future or confronting the grand challenges of our time through dauntless research. UG educates more than 38,000students annually, with approximately 2.64% being computer science students. We turn ideas into impact and transform the world around us.
